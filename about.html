At Glen Harvest, we believe snacking should be a ritual of wellness, not guilt. We source our makhana (fox nuts) directly from the lotus ponds of Bihar, ensuring every batch meets the highest standards of organic farming.

Our slow-roasting process preserves the crunch and locks in the nutrients, creating a snack that is not only devastatingly delicious but entirely good for you.
